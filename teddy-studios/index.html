About TEDDY STUDIOS

Capturing Moments. Creating Stories. Inspiring Audiences.

Founded by THADEUS ONDARI, TEDDY STUDIOS is a professional creative media company dedicated to transforming ideas into powerful visual and audio experiences.

We specialize in photography, videography, audio production, live streaming, branding and digital content creation.
